Why Our Idea ?

"If you don't solve a problem someone else will"
Around 250 million students across the world in lockwon with countless more working from home. This has created huge problems and there is not any viable video conferencing solution with privacy concerns for some. So , our team with help from our patreons has come up with a solution

What is our idea ?

We have developed a video conferencing software especially for remote areas like villages. It uses low bandwith (0.3gb/hr) and has low software requirements to install.It can be installed on Andorid(4 and above) , Windows (XP and above). It is free to download and we plan to launch to more software with help from our patreons and government. Contribute to this nobel cause.
